Health


Disorders


Anxiety Disorder - Anxiety disorder is when someone feels irrational dread while doing everyday activities. According to SDAA, “Anxiety disorders are the most common mental illness in the U.S., affecting 40 million adults in the United States age 18 and older. Only about one-third of those suffering from an anxiety disorder receive treatment, even though the disorders are highly treatable”.

Depression - Depression is a serious medical illness where people will feel sad and hopeless for long periods of time. Depression can cause people to not feel interested in the things they once loved.

Eating Disorders - This is one of the biggest problems here in the United States. Teens and young adults are more likely to develop eating disorder at the age of 18-19. Eating disorder occurs when a person has a low self-esteem, financial problems, and etc.

Suicide - Suicide is when someone has the urge to end themselves. And suicide is increasing day by day. The most common age for suicide is 40-50 yrs old. Too much stress and self-evaluation daily can cause you think about suicide. Contact a doctor immediately if you or someone you know has these types of thoughts.
